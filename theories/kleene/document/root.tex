\title{Kleene Algebra in Unifying Theories of Programming}

\author{Simon Foster}

\maketitle

\begin{abstract}
  This development links Isabelle/UTP to the mechanised Kleene Algebra (KA) hiearchy for Isabelle/HOL. We substantiate
  the required KA laws, and provides a large body of additional theorems for alphabetised relations which are provided
  by the KA library. Additionally, we show how such theorems can be lifted to a subclass of UTP theories, provided
  certain conditions hold.
\end{abstract}
